Role Summary

As a CRM/System Specialist (Trainee) in our IT Department, you will be the expert behind the systems that drive our client relationships and business processes. This is a foundational role for a tech-savvy individual passionate about business automation and data management. You will receive extensive training on leading CRM platforms like Zoho, HubSpot, and Salesforce, and you will learn to implement, customize, and manage these systems to optimize our sales, marketing, and customer support functions. This role offers a clear career path toward becoming an expert in business automation technology.

Key Responsibilities

  • CRM Setup & Implementation: Assist in the configuration and customization of CRM platforms (Zoho, HubSpot, Salesforce) for clients and internal teams.
  • Process Automation: Design and implement automated workflows, email sequences, and lead nurturing campaigns.
  • System Integration: Support the integration of CRM systems with other business platforms using APIs, webhooks, and tools like Zapier.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Help build and maintain business intelligence dashboards to track sales performance, customer engagement, and other KPIs.
  • Data Management: Assist with data cleanup, security protocols, and general system administration to ensure data integrity.
  • Training & Support: Help develop training materials and provide support to users to ensure effective adoption of CRM systems.

Required Technical Skills

Core Skills

  • Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology, Business, or a related field.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Familiarity with CRM concepts and database management (SQL).
  • Basic understanding of web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).

Preferred Skills

  • Hands-on experience with a major CRM platform (Zoho, HubSpot, Salesforce).
  • Knowledge of integration tools like Zapier or Microsoft Power Automate.
  • Experience with business intelligence tools like Power BI or Looker Studio.

Compensation & Growth

  • Month 1 (Retention Period): A performance-based stipend will be provided for October 2025.
  • From November 1st: The role transitions to a full-time salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month.
  • Performance Incentives: Eligible for monthly performance bonuses of ₹3,000 - ₹7,000.
  • Career Pathway: A structured progression from Trainee → Associate → Assistant Executive → Executive → Department Head, with a potential 50% salary increase within the first year based on performance.
  • Benefits: PF registration, statutory benefits, and access to premium CRM platforms and professional certifications.
