SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of optimizing your website to rank higher organically (for free) in search results. SEM (Search Engine Marketing) is a broader term, but most often refers to paid advertising on search engines, like Google Ads. SEO is a long-term strategy, while SEM can deliver immediate traffic.

We measure Return on Investment (ROI) by tracking key metrics tied to your business goals. This includes metrics like Cost Per Lead (CPL), Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), and Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).
