Position Overview

As an AI Marketing Content Creator Intern, you will be at the forefront of modern marketing, blending creativity with cutting-edge technology. This 3-month internship in our Marketing Department offers a unique opportunity to learn and apply AI-powered tools to create compelling content that drives engagement and results. You will work on real client projects across diverse industries, receiving mentorship and hands-on training in a dynamic, innovative environment. This role is a direct pathway to a potential full-time position, aligning with our mission to empower the next generation of marketing professionals.

Key Responsibilities

  • AI-Powered Content Creation: Utilize AI tools to generate, refine, and optimize content for blogs, social media, websites, and email campaigns.
  • Digital Marketing: Assist in executing digital marketing strategies, including SEO-focused content, social media updates, and ad copy.
  • Market Research: Use AI-driven insights to conduct market research, analyze competitor content, and identify new content opportunities.
  • Client Deliverables: Support the creation of client-focused marketing materials, ensuring content is aligned with brand voice and strategic goals.
  • Performance Analysis: Help track and analyze content performance metrics to inform future content strategies.

Skills & Qualifications

Required Skills

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Marketing, Communications, Journalism, or a related field.
  • Exceptional writing, editing, and storytelling skills.
  • A strong interest in artificial intelligence and its application in marketing.
  • Familiarity with digital marketing channels and social media platforms.

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with AI content generation tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Jasper, Copy.ai).
  • Basic understanding of SEO principles.
  • A portfolio of writing samples or content creation projects.
  • Ability to adapt to new technologies and learn quickly.

Compensation & Growth

  • Compensation: This is an unpaid internship focused on providing a structured learning experience with cutting-edge AI tools.
  • Performance Incentives: Exceptional performers may be eligible for performance-based incentives.
  • Pre-Placement Offer (PPO): High-performing interns will be strongly considered for a full-time Trainee position upon successful completion of the internship.
  • Future Role: The full-time Trainee role offers a competitive starting salary and a clear career progression pathway within our organization.
  • Professional Growth: Gain invaluable hands-on experience with AI marketing, receive dedicated mentorship, and build a portfolio that sets you apart.

Application Process

  1. Application Submission: Apply via the link below by September 25th, 2025.
  2. HR Screening Call: A preliminary discussion about your interest in AI and content creation.
  3. Practical Assessment & Interview: An interview with the Marketing Department that may include a short content creation task.
  4. Final Interview: A final conversation with Senior Leadership.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: Successful candidates will receive an offer for a October 1st, 2025 start date.
