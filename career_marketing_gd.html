Position Overview

As a Graphic Designer Intern in our Marketing Department, you will be a key creative contributor, helping to shape the visual identity of RIG Global Solutions and our diverse clients. This 3-month, project-based internship is an excellent opportunity for a talented and aspiring designer to gain hands-on experience in a professional setting. You will work on a wide range of projects, from branding and digital marketing to client presentations, all while receiving mentorship from our senior team. This internship is your gateway to a potential full-time role and a structured career in design and marketing.

Key Responsibilities

  • Visual Design: Create engaging graphics for social media, websites, email campaigns, and presentations.
  • Branding: Assist in the development and application of brand guidelines for both internal and client projects.
  • Digital Marketing Support: Design visual assets for digital ad campaigns, content marketing, and other online initiatives.
  • Client Support: Collaborate with our consulting teams to create professional and visually compelling client deliverables.
  • Template Creation: Develop and maintain a library of design templates for presentations, reports, and marketing materials.

Skills & Qualifications

Technical Skills

  • Proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign).
  • Experience with Canva and Figma or similar design tools.
  • A strong portfolio showcasing your design skills and creativity.
  • Basic understanding of design principles, typography, and color theory.

Soft Skills

  • A creative and innovative mindset.
  • Excellent attention to detail.
  • Ability to manage time effectively and meet deadlines.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills.

Compensation & Growth

  • Compensation: This is an unpaid internship focused on learning and development.
  • Performance Incentives: Exceptional performers who demonstrate extraordinary skill and commitment may be eligible for performance-based incentives ranging from ₹2,000 - ₹5,000.
  • Pre-Placement Offer (PPO): High-performing interns will be given priority consideration for a full-time Trainee position upon successful completion of the internship.
  • Future Role: The full-time Trainee role comes with a starting salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month and a clear career progression pathway.
  • Professional Growth: Gain invaluable hands-on experience, receive mentorship from senior designers, and build a professional portfolio.

Application Process

  1. Application Submission: Apply via the link below by September 25th, 2025. A portfolio is required.
  2. HR Screening Call: A preliminary discussion about your creative background and career aspirations.
  3. Portfolio Review & Interview: An interview with the Marketing Department to discuss your portfolio and design skills.
  4. Final Interview: A final conversation with Senior Leadership.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: Successful candidates will receive an offer for a October 1st, 2025 start date.
