Job Opening: Business Strategy Consultant (Trainee)

Consultancy Department | Full-Time Virtual

Start Date

September 1st, 2025

Schedule

Mon-Sat, 10 AM - 6 PM

Role Summary

As a Business Strategy Consultant (Trainee), you will be part of our dynamic Consultancy Department, focusing on business strategy, client solutions, and strategic planning. This role offers an exceptional opportunity to work on diverse client projects ranging from startups to established enterprises, while developing expertise in strategic consulting and business transformation. You will support senior consultants in delivering high-impact strategic solutions, conduct comprehensive business research, and contribute to developing innovative frameworks and models for our diverse client portfolio.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct comprehensive industry and client-specific business research, including competitive landscape analysis and market trend studies.
  • Assist in developing strategic roadmaps (3-year/5-year), business model canvases, and go-to-market strategies for clients.
  • Contribute to SWOT, PESTEL, and other strategic analyses to support client planning and decision-making.
  • Help prepare client deliverables, including presentations, strategic models, investor pitch decks, and comprehensive reports.
  • Support project coordination, maintain documentation, and assist in client communication to ensure project milestones are met.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support process mapping, cost optimization, and digital transformation strategy projects.

Qualifications

Required

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration, Management, Economics, Commerce, or a related field.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, PowerPoint, Word).
  • Strong analytical, research, and problem-solving capabilities.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams with a client-centric approach.

Preferred

  • MBA/PGDM students or graduates.
  • Knowledge of business intelligence tools (Power BI, Tableau).
  • Understanding of the Indian startup ecosystem and MSME sector.
  • Familiarity with strategic frameworks (e.g., SWOT, Porter's Five Forces).

Compensation & Growth

  • Initial Stipend: A performance-based stipend will be provided for the month of September 2025 based on project delivery and KPI achievement.
  • Full Salary: From October 1st, 2025, the role transitions to a full-time salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month.
  • Performance Incentives: Eligible for monthly performance bonuses ranging from ₹3,000 - ₹7,000.
  • Guaranteed Growth: A 50% salary hike potential within the first year based on bi-annual performance reviews.
  • Benefits: PF registration and all statutory employment benefits are included.
  • Career Pathway: Trainee (18 months) → Associate (12 months) → Assistant Executive (12 months) → Executive → Department Head.
  • Professional Development: Continuous training, mentorship from senior consultants, and exposure to diverse, high-impact client projects.

Application Process

  1. Application Submission: Apply via the link below by August 30th, 2025. Your application should include an updated Resume/CV and a Cover Letter.
  2. HR Screening Call: A preliminary discussion about your background and interest in strategic consulting.
  3. Technical Interview: An assessment with the Consultancy Department team, which may include a case study presentation.
  4. Final Interview: A final conversation with the Department Head.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: Successful candidates will receive an offer for a September 1st, 2025 start date.
