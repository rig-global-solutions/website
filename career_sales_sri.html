Position Overview

As a Sales Representative Intern, you will embark on a 3-month journey to become a skilled B2B sales professional. This role is designed for ambitious and driven individuals who want to learn the art of consultative selling in a dynamic, multi-service environment. You will receive comprehensive training and mentorship while actively contributing to our sales pipeline. This internship provides hands-on experience with our diverse client base—from startups and MSMEs to corporate and government entities—and offers a direct pathway to a full-time career for high performers.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead Generation: Proactively identify and qualify potential B2B leads using platforms like LinkedIn Sales Navigator, JustDial, and Upwork.
  • Client Outreach: Engage prospective clients through email, cold calling, and social media to introduce our comprehensive service portfolio.
  • CRM Management: Maintain and manage the sales pipeline with precision using our professional CRM system.
  • Sales Support: Assist the senior sales team in preparing proposals, presentations, and client communications.
  • Market Analysis: Research and understand the needs of our target markets to tailor sales pitches effectively.
  • Relationship Building: Learn to build and nurture professional relationships with business decision-makers across various industries.

Skills & Qualifications

Required Skills

  • Currently pursuing or recently completed a Bachelor's degree.
  • Exceptional communication and interpersonal skills.
  • A high level of motivation, resilience, and a strong desire to succeed in a sales environment.
  • Ability to learn quickly and manage time effectively.

Preferred Skills

  • Prior experience or internship in a sales or customer-facing role.
  • Familiarity with CRM software and B2B lead generation platforms.
  • A competitive spirit and a goal-oriented mindset.

Compensation & Growth

  • Compensation: This is an unpaid internship focused on intensive training and skill development in professional B2B sales.
  • Performance Incentives: Exceptional performers who consistently exceed targets will be eligible for performance-based incentives ranging from ₹7,500 - ₹20,000.
  • Pre-Placement Offer (PPO): High-performing interns will be given priority consideration for a full-time Sales Representative (Trainee) position.
  • Future Role: The full-time Trainee role offers a competitive salary, full benefits, and a clear, performance-driven career path.
  • Professional Growth: Receive comprehensive training in consultative selling, CRM management, and our diverse service portfolio, along with dedicated mentorship from experienced sales leaders.

Application Process

  1. Application Submission: Apply via the link below by September 25th, 2025.
  2. HR Screening Call: A preliminary discussion to assess your motivation and fit for a sales role.
  3. Sales Aptitude Interview: An interview with the Sales Department Head to evaluate your potential and communication skills.
  4. Final Interview: A final conversation with Senior Leadership.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: Successful candidates will receive an offer for a October 1st, 2025 start date.
