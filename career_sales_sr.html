Role Summary

As a Sales Representative (Trainee), you will be on the front lines of RIG Global Solutions' growth engine. This role is designed for a motivated and ambitious individual eager to start a career in sales and business development. You will learn the art and science of consultative selling, from generating leads and managing a CRM to building client relationships and closing deals. This position offers extensive training and a clear, performance-driven path to advancement within our structured career framework.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead Generation: Identify and qualify potential clients through research, networking, and outreach.
  • CRM Management: Maintain an accurate and up-to-date pipeline of leads and opportunities in our CRM system.
  • Client Engagement: Conduct initial discovery calls to understand client needs and effectively communicate the value of our services.
  • Sales Support: Assist senior sales team members in preparing proposals, presentations, and contracts.
  • Relationship Building: Nurture relationships with prospective clients to build a strong foundation for long-term partnerships.
  • Market Feedback: Provide insights from client interactions to the marketing and R&D teams to help refine our service offerings.

Qualifications

Required

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business, Marketing, or a related field.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • A strong desire to build a career in sales.
  • Self-motivated with a proactive and resilient attitude.
  • Strong interpersonal and relationship-building skills.

Preferred

  • Internship or prior experience in a sales or customer-facing role.
  • Familiarity with CRM software (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot).
  • A basic understanding of consulting or professional services.

Compensation & Growth

  • October 2025 (Retention Period): A performance-based stipend will be provided.
  • From November 2025: The role transitions to a full-time salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month.
  • Performance Incentives: Eligible for monthly performance bonuses of ₹3,000 - ₹7,000.
  • Career Pathway: A clear progression from Trainee → Associate → Assistant Executive → Executive.
  • Benefits: PF registration, statutory benefits, and access to our comprehensive sales training programs.

Application Process

  1. Application Submission: Apply via the link below by September 25th, 2025.
  2. HR Screening Call: A preliminary call to discuss your interest in a sales career.
  3. Sales Aptitude Interview: An interview with the Sales Department Head to assess your potential.
  4. Final Interview: A final conversation with Senior Leadership.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: Successful candidates will receive an offer for a October 1st, 2025 start date.
