Role Summary

As a Senior Legal Consultant (Trainee) in our Advisory Department, you will provide critical legal and regulatory guidance to our diverse portfolio of clients. This entry-level role is a unique opportunity for a legally-minded individual to develop into a strategic business advisor. You will work at the intersection of law and business, helping startups, MSMEs, and corporate clients navigate complex compliance landscapes, manage contracts, and structure their operations for success, all while being supported by a robust mentorship and training program.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in providing legal and regulatory advisory services to clients, focusing on corporate law, compliance, and contract management.
  • Support clients with company registration processes (LLP, Pvt Ltd, etc.) and ensure adherence to ROC compliance and annual filing requirements.
  • Conduct research on regulatory frameworks from bodies like SEBI, RBI, and IRDA to guide client strategy.
  • Draft, review, and help negotiate commercial contracts, agreements, and other legal documents.
  • Collaborate with the Finance and Consultancy teams to provide integrated solutions that address both legal and business challenges.
  • Maintain up-to-date knowledge of changes in corporate law and advise on their implications for clients and the firm.

Qualifications

Required

  • Bachelor's Degree in Law (LLB) or a related field.
  • Strong understanding of Indian corporate and contract law.
  • Excellent analytical, research, and drafting skills.
  • High ethical standards and a commitment to professional integrity.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite.

Preferred

  • Pursuing or completed a Master's Degree in Law (LLM) or Company Secretary (CS) qualification.
  • Experience or internship in a corporate law firm or in-house legal department.
  • Familiarity with the legal challenges faced by startups and MSMEs.
  • Strong communication and client-facing skills.

Compensation & Growth

  • Initial Stipend: A performance-based stipend will be provided for the 1-month retention period in September 2025.
  • Full Salary: From October 1st, 2025, the role transitions to a full-time salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month.
  • Performance Incentives: Eligible for monthly performance bonuses based on KPIs.
  • Guaranteed Growth: A clear, performance-based career progression pathway from Trainee to Executive.
  • Benefits: PF registration and all statutory employment benefits are included.
  • Professional Development: Continuous training in both legal and business strategy, with mentorship from senior leaders and opportunities for cross-functional collaboration.

Application Process

  3. Technical Interview: An assessment of your legal knowledge and problem-solving skills with the Advisory Department.
  4. Final Interview: A final conversation with a Department Head.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: Successful candidates will receive an offer for a September 1st, 2025 start date.
