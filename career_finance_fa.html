Position Overview

As a Financial Analyst Trainee at RIG Global Solutions, you will be at the heart of our Finance Department, playing a crucial role in delivering excellence to our diverse client base. This is an entry-level position designed for ambitious individuals eager to build a foundational career in finance. You will receive comprehensive training and mentorship, working on live projects that support individuals, startups, and MSMEs. This role is your first step on a guaranteed, performance-based career path from Trainee to Executive.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage and maintain accurate financial records for our clients using tools like Tally and Excel.
  • Perform regular reconciliation of bank statements, accounts payable, and accounts receivable.
  • Assist in the preparation of financial reports, including monthly profit & loss statements and balance sheets.
  • Support senior analysts in providing compliance services related to GST, TDS, and other statutory requirements.
  • Engage in client service delivery, addressing queries and providing support under the guidance of a mentor.
  • Contribute to the development of financial models and analyses to support client decision-making.

Qualifications

Required

  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, Commerce, or a related field.
  • Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel and Tally.
  • Basic knowledge of GST and TDS compliance.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with high attention to detail.

Preferred

  • Currently pursuing professional certifications (e.g., CA, CMA).
  • Familiarity with Business Intelligence (BI) tools.
  • An understanding of the startup and MSME ecosystem in India.
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.

Compensation & Benefits

  • Initial Stipend: A performance-based stipend will be provided for the month of September 2025.
  • Full Salary: From October 1st, 2025, the role transitions to a full-time salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month.
  • Performance Incentives: Eligible for monthly performance bonuses ranging from ₹3,000 - ₹7,000.
  • Guaranteed Growth: A 50% salary hike potential within the first year based on bi-annual performance reviews.
  • Benefits: PF registration and all statutory employment benefits are included.
  • Career Pathway: This role is the first step in our 6-level career progression system, leading to Associate, Assistant Executive, and Executive roles.
  • Training & Culture: Access to our comprehensive, free training and mentorship programs in a supportive, youth-focused environment.

Application Process

  1. Application Submission: Apply via the link below by August 30th, 2025.
  2. HR Screening: A preliminary call to discuss your background and alignment with our values.
  3. Technical Interview: An assessment of your financial and accounting knowledge.
  4. Behavioral Interview: A final conversation with a department leader.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: Successful candidates will receive an offer, with onboarding beginning September 1st, 2025.
