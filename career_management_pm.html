Job Opening: Project Manager (Trainee)

Management Department | 100% Virtual/Remote

Start Date

October 1st, 2025

Schedule

Mon-Fri, 10 AM - 6 PM

Application Deadline

September 25th, 2025

Role Summary

As a Project Manager (Trainee) in our Management Department, you will be the central force driving our client projects from conception to successful completion. This role is designed for a highly organized and motivated individual ready to build a career in project management. You will learn to oversee project timelines, coordinate across our 10 specialized departments, manage performance dashboards, and ensure flawless delivery, making you a critical link between our internal teams and our clients.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor project timelines and ensure all milestones are met according to the project plan.
  • Coordinate workflows and communication between cross-departmental teams to ensure seamless project execution.
  • Maintain and update performance dashboards to track key project metrics and provide regular status reports to clients and leadership.
  • Organize project meetings, set agendas, and manage action items to keep all stakeholders aligned.
  • Act as a key point of contact for client inquiries, ensuring transparent communication and tracking project deliverables.
  • Support senior managers in risk assessment and mitigation planning for all assigned projects.

Qualifications

Required

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business, Management, or a related field.
  • Exceptional organizational and time-management skills.
  • Strong written and verbal communication abilities.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite or similar project management tools.
  • A proactive and collaborative mindset with a focus on problem-solving.

Preferred

  • Familiarity with project management methodologies (e.g., Agile, Scrum).
  • Experience in a client-facing or coordination role.
  • Professional certifications in project management (e.g., CAPM) are a plus.

Compensation & Growth

  • Phase 1 (Retention Period): A performance-based stipend will be provided for October 2025.
  • Phase 2 (Full-Time): From November 1st, 2025, the role transitions to a full-time salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month.
  • Performance Incentives: Eligible for monthly performance bonuses of ₹3,000 - ₹7,000 based on KPIs.
  • Guaranteed Growth: A 50% salary increase potential within the first year based on bi-annual performance reviews.
  • Career Pathway: Clear progression from Trainee → Associate → Assistant Executive → Executive.
  • Benefits: PF registration, statutory benefits, health insurance, and continuous professional development.

Application Process

  1. Application Submission: Apply via the link below by September 25th, 2025.
  2. HR Screening Call (30 minutes): A preliminary discussion about your skills and career goals.
  3. Technical Interview (60 minutes): An in-depth interview with the Management Department Head.
  4. Final Behavioral Interview (45 minutes): A final conversation with Senior Leadership.
  5. Offer & Onboarding: Successful candidates will undergo pre-employment verification and receive an offer for a October 1st, 2025 start date.
