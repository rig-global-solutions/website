Role Summary

As a Web Developer (Trainee) in our IT Department, you will be instrumental in building the digital solutions that power our firm and our clients. This is a foundational role for an aspiring full-stack developer who is passionate about technology and eager to learn. You will receive extensive training and mentorship while working on end-to-end web application development, from crafting responsive frontends with modern frameworks to building robust backends and managing databases. This role places you on a fast-track career path in a cutting-edge technology environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Frontend Development: Develop responsive and user-friendly interfaces using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React.js.
  • Backend Development: Build and maintain server-side logic, databases, and APIs using languages such as Node.js, Python, or PHP.
  • Full-Stack Applications: Participate in the entire application lifecycle, focusing on coding, debugging, and building scalable solutions.
  • CMS & E-Commerce: Assist in the development and maintenance of websites on platforms like WordPress, WooCommerce, and Shopify.
  • Database Management: Support the design and management of databases like MySQL and MongoDB to ensure data integrity and performance.
  • DevOps & Deployment: Gain exposure to CI/CD pipelines and cloud platforms like AWS and Azure for deploying and managing applications.
  • Website Maintenance: Assist with ongoing website updates, performance tuning, and SEO optimization.

Required Technical Skills

Frontend

  • Solid understanding of HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (ES6+).
  • Familiarity with at least one modern frontend framework (React, Angular, or Vue.js).

Backend

  • Basic knowledge of a server-side language (e.g., Node.js, Python, PHP).
  • Understanding of database systems (SQL or NoSQL) and API development (REST).

General

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related field.
  • Proficiency with Git version control.
  • Strong problem-solving and debugging skills.

Compensation & Growth

  • Month 1 (Retention Period): A performance-based stipend will be provided for October 2025.
  • From November 1st: The role transitions to a full-time salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month.
  • Performance Incentives: Eligible for monthly performance bonuses of ₹3,000 - ₹7,000.
  • Career Pathway: A structured progression from Trainee → Associate → Assistant Executive → Executive → Department Head, with a potential 50% salary increase within the first year based on performance.
  • Benefits: PF registration, statutory benefits, and access to premium development tools, training, and cloud platform certifications (AWS, Azure).
