A CMS (Content Management System) is software that allows you to manage your website's content easily. You need one if you plan to update your site regularly (e.g., adding blog posts, changing service descriptions, or updating team members). It gives you control over your site without needing to contact a developer for every small change.

A static website has fixed content, and changes require editing the code directly. It's great for simple portfolios or brochure sites.
