Frequently Asked Questions

What is the eligibility criteria for a personal loan? + –

Eligibility depends on your age, income, credit score, and employment status. Generally, you need to be a salaried employee or self-employed with a stable income.

What documents are required for application? + –

Commonly required documents include ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN), address proof, latest salary slips, 3-6 months bank statements, and employment proof.

How long does it take to get the loan approved? + –

With our instant matching, you get pre-approval offers instantly. After document verification, the final disbursal can happen in as little as 24-48 hours.

Is your consultation service really free? + – Yes, 100%. We offer full application assistance and consultation at zero extra cost to you. We receive our commission from the lender you choose, so our service is completely free for you.
