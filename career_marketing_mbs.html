Role Summary

As a Marketing & Branding Specialist (Trainee), you will be a creative force within our Marketing Department, responsible for shaping the voice and identity of RIG Global Solutions and our clients. This role is perfect for a recent graduate passionate about storytelling, digital marketing, and brand strategy. You will receive hands-on training and mentorship while working on a wide array of tasks, from developing brand strategies and creating digital content to managing social media and executing marketing campaigns.

Key Responsibilities

  • Brand Strategy & Identity: Assist in developing brand guidelines, messaging, and identity for clients and internal projects.
  • Digital Marketing & Content: Create compelling content for blogs, websites, and social media; support SEO and email marketing campaigns.
  • Social Media Management: Manage and grow our social media presence across platforms like LinkedIn, creating engaging posts and analyzing performance.
  • Campaign Development: Support the planning, execution, and monitoring of integrated marketing campaigns from start to finish.
  • Market Research: Conduct market and competitor analysis to identify trends and inform marketing strategies.
  • Client Support: Collaborate with the consultancy and sales teams to develop marketing materials and support client projects.

Qualifications

Required

  • Bachelor's Degree in Marketing, Communications, Business, or a related field.
  • Excellent writing, editing, and communication skills.
  • A creative mindset with a strong eye for design and detail.
  • Familiarity with major social media platforms and digital marketing concepts.
  • Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced virtual environment.

Preferred

  • Internship or experience in a marketing or branding role.
  • Experience with content creation tools (e.g., Canva, Adobe Suite).
  • Basic knowledge of SEO, SEM, and Google Analytics.
  • A portfolio showcasing writing samples or marketing projects.

Compensation & Growth

  • September 2025 (Retention Period): A performance-based stipend will be provided.
  • From October 2025: The role transitions to a full-time salary of ₹20,000 - ₹30,000 per month.
  • Performance Incentives: Eligible for monthly performance bonuses of ₹3,000 - ₹7,000 based on KPIs.
  • Career Pathway: A clear, performance-based progression from Trainee to Executive level.
  • Benefits: Comprehensive benefits package including PF, statutory benefits, and continuous learning opportunities.
